How BELG SERVICES Works

A simple and fast way to connect clients with workers

1

Create Your Account

Sign up as a client, worker, or company. The registration process is simple and takes less than one minute.

2

Choose the Service You Need

Browse a wide range of services such as cleaning, moving, repairs, renovation, gardening, and more.

3

Instant Matching

The platform automatically finds available workers near your location based on skills, ratings, and availability.

4

Confirm & Start the Job

Once you choose a worker, you can chat, confirm the details, and start the job immediately.

5

Secure Payment

Payments are processed safely through the platform with full transparency and no hidden fees.

6

Rate the Service

After the job is completed, rate the worker or company. This helps maintain quality and trust across the platform.
