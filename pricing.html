Pricing & Commission System

Simple, transparent, and fair for everyone

Commission System

BELG SERVICES uses a simple percentage-based model. Both clients and workers pay a small 5% fee only after the job is completed. Companies do not pay commission — they use monthly subscription plans.

Commission Fees

Category Fee Notes
Clients 5% Paid after job completion
Workers 5% Deducted after job completion
Companies 0% Companies use subscription plans instead
