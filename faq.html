Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions

What is BELG SERVICES?
BELG SERVICES is a platform that connects clients with professional workers for services such as cleaning, moving, repairs, renovation, and more.
How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking on the Register page and filling out your basic information. The process takes less than one minute.
Is the service available in all cities?
The platform is expanding quickly and aims to cover all major cities in Belgium.
How are workers selected?
Workers are verified based on identity, experience, and customer ratings to ensure high-quality service.
How do payments work?
Payments are processed securely through the platform with full transparency and no hidden fees.
Can I cancel a booking?
Yes, you can cancel a booking before the worker starts the job. Cancellation rules may apply depending on the service.
